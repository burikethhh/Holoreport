Everything You Need

A complete gesture-controlled presentation system, fully offline.

✋

Hand Gesture Control

Navigate slides with natural swipe gestures using your webcam. Powered by MediaPipe hand tracking — no extra hardware needed.

🔮

Laser Pointer

Use your index finger as a virtual laser pointer with 11 customizable pointer styles. Point at anything on screen.

🔌

Fully Offline

Works without internet. All AI models, fonts, and assets are bundled. Perfect for secure environments and presentations on the go.

📄

PowerPoint Support

Drag-and-drop your .pptx files. Slides are rendered with high fidelity using PowerPoint COM, LibreOffice, or built-in XML parser.

👥

User Tracking

Know who uses your presentations. Built-in welcome screen captures user names with a secure admin dashboard.

🔒

Secured & Protected

Source code is obfuscated, packed in ASAR archive. Admin dashboard requires authentication. Your content stays safe.
