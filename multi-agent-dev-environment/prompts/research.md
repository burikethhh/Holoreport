# Research Phase Prompts

## Objectives
- Identify key areas of research relevant to the project.
- Gather data and insights that will inform the development process.

## Prompts
1. **Topic Exploration**: What are the current trends and technologies in [specific area of interest]? Provide a summary of recent advancements.
   
2. **Literature Review**: Find and summarize three recent research papers related to [specific topic]. What are their key findings and implications for our project?

3. **Competitive Analysis**: Identify three competitors in the [specific domain]. What are their strengths and weaknesses? How can we differentiate our project?

4. **User Needs Assessment**: What are the primary needs and pain points of our target users? Conduct surveys or interviews to gather qualitative data.

5. **Data Sources**: List potential data sources that can be utilized for our research. Include both primary and secondary sources.

6. **Feasibility Study**: Assess the feasibility of implementing [specific feature or technology]. What are the potential challenges and how can they be addressed?

7. **Best Practices**: What are the best practices in [specific area]? How can we incorporate these into our project to enhance its effectiveness?

8. **Future Trends**: What emerging trends should we be aware of that could impact our project in the next 3-5 years? 

## Deliverables
- A comprehensive report summarizing findings from the above prompts.
- A presentation to share insights with the team, highlighting key research outcomes and recommendations.
