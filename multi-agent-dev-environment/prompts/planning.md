# Planning Phase Prompts and Guidelines

## Objectives
1. Define the overall goals of the project.
2. Identify key deliverables and milestones.
3. Establish timelines for each phase of development.

## Strategies
- Collaborate with all agents to gather input on project objectives.
- Utilize brainstorming sessions to generate ideas and solutions.
- Prioritize tasks based on impact and feasibility.

## Questions to Consider
- What are the primary problems we aim to solve with this project?
- Who are the target users, and what are their needs?
- What resources (time, tools, personnel) are required for successful execution?

## Action Items
- Create a detailed project timeline with deadlines for each milestone.
- Assign roles and responsibilities to each agent for the planning phase.
- Schedule regular check-ins to assess progress and adjust plans as necessary.

## Documentation
- Maintain a record of all planning discussions and decisions.
- Update the project roadmap as objectives evolve.
- Ensure all agents have access to planning documents for transparency and collaboration.
